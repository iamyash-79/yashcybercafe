Shipping & Delivery

At YourStore, we are committed to providing you with a seamless shopping experience, which includes reliable and efficient shipping and delivery services.

Shipping Methods & Times

We offer various shipping methods to meet your needs. The estimated delivery times and costs will vary depending on your location and the selected shipping option at checkout.

  • Standard Shipping: Estimated delivery within 5-7 business days.
  • Express Shipping: Estimated delivery within 2-3 business days.
  • Local Pickup: Available for select items at our designated pickup points. (Please check product pages for eligibility.)

Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated based on the weight, dimensions, and destination of your order. You can view the exact shipping cost for your order during the checkout process before finalizing your purchase.

We occasionally offer free shipping promotions. Keep an eye on our homepage or subscribe to our newsletter for updates on special offers!

Order Processing Time

Orders are typically processed and dispatched within 1-2 business days (Monday-Friday, excluding public holidays) after payment confirmation. You will receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking information once your order has been dispatched.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order is shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number and a link to track your package's journey. You can also track your order directly from your "My Orders" page after logging into your account.

International Shipping

We currently ship to a limited number of international destinations. Please check the shipping options available at checkout for your country. International customers are responsible for any customs duties, taxes, or fees imposed by their respective countries.

Damaged or Lost Packages

While we take utmost care in packaging and shipping your items, unforeseen circumstances can occur. If your package arrives damaged or is lost in transit, please contact our customer support team immediately at support@yourstore.com. We will work diligently to resolve the issue for you.
