Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

To place an order, simply browse our shop, add desired items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. Follow the steps to provide shipping information and select your payment method.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept UPI and Cash on Delivery (COD). Credit/Debit card payments are currently disabled.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number and a link to track your package. You can also find tracking information on your "My Orders" page.

What is your return policy?

Our return policy allows returns within 30 days of purchase for unused items in their original packaging. Please refer to our Return & Refund Policy page for full details.

How do I change my shipping address?

You can change your shipping address during the checkout process when placing a new order. For existing orders, please contact our customer support team immediately.

Do you offer international shipping?

We currently ship to a limited number of international destinations. Please check the shipping options available at checkout for your country.
